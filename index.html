Premium Quality lubricants, trusted worldwide since 2007. Partner with Boerjia for reliability, innovation, and excellence.

Welcome to Shandong Boerjia Petrochemical Technology Co., Ltd. For over 15 years, we have been at the forefront of lubricant innovation, serving customers across Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Africa, and beyond.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities span over 50,000 square meters, with an annual production capacity exceeding 50,000 tons. Every product that leaves our facility undergoes rigorous quality control testing in our ISO-certified laboratory.

At Boerjia, we specialize in a comprehensive range of lubricants including engine oils, hydraulic oils, gear oils, brake fluids, and specialty greases. Our dedicated R&D team continuously develops new formulations to meet the evolving demands of modern engines and machinery.

We take pride in our OEM and ODM capabilities, offering customized solutions tailored to your specific requirements. Whether you need a proprietary brand or specialized formulation, our technical team is ready to bring your vision to life.

With products exported to over 30 countries and a commitment to on-time delivery, Boerjia has established itself as a trusted partner for businesses worldwide. Experience the Boerjia difference today.
