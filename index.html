About BOERJIA

Shandong Boerjia Petrochemical Technology Co., Ltd., established in 2007, is a professional lubricant manufacturer integrating R&D, production, and sales. With over 15 years of industry experience, we specialize in engine oil, hydraulic oil, gear oil, brake fluid, lubricating grease, and marine oil.

Our products meet international standards including API, ILSAC, ISO, and have been exported to 30+ countries across Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Africa, and South America. We provide full OEM/ODM services with flexible MOQ and fast delivery.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Our standard MOQ is 500 liters for bulk orders or 200 bottles for packaged products. For trial orders, we can accept smaller quantities. Contact us for details.
Do you provide OEM/ODM services?
Yes! We offer full OEM/ODM services including custom formulation, private labeling, custom packaging design, and brand-specific quality standards. Our R&D team can develop formulations to meet your market requirements.
What certifications do your products have?
Our products are certified to API, ILSAC, ISO, and other international standards. We hold ISO 9001:2015 quality management certification. All products undergo rigorous testing in our in-house laboratory.
How long does delivery take?
Standard production lead time is 7-15 working days after order confirmation. Shipping time depends on destination: approximately 15-25 days by sea to Southeast Asia, 25-35 days to Middle East/Africa, and 35-45 days to South America.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept T/T (Telegraphic Transfer), L/C (Letter of Credit), and Western Union. For new customers, a 30% deposit with 70% before shipment is standard. We can discuss flexible terms for long-term partnerships.
Can I get free samples?
Yes, we provide free samples (up to 1L per product) for serious buyers. Sample shipping cost is typically borne by the buyer. Contact us with your requirements and we will arrange sample delivery.
